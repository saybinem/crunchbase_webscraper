Update

He is the mastermind of the MyCheck platform and is the one who makes Shlomit and Tal’s ideas a reality . Erez has over 15 years experience in database design and integration, which included project management work for one of the most mission-critical programs within the Israel Defense Forces. Erez was Tal’s co-founder at the Atarim Group, where he served as Chief Operating Officer for 4 years. In 2008, Erez was recognized by Forbes Magazine as one of the top 300 most influential people in Israel under 40. Erez has a Bachelor’s Degree in Economics and Management and a Computer Science Degree from Open University in Israel.
